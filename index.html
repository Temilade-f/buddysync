You can delete your BuddySync account at any time directly from within the app.

To delete your account:

  1. Open the BuddySync app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm your choice

What happens after deletion?

Once deleted, all your data (profile, messages, tags, preferences, etc.) will be permanently removed from our systems within 7 days.
